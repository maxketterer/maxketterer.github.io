About

At Willows & Wine, we offer hand-picked local and international wine, cocktails and spirits with charcuterie boards and artisan cheese platters. What started as an idea became reality when we lovingly renovated the former Spanish bodega to open W&W in 2016. Enjoy the food & drink, read a pre-loved book or play a board game - we'd love to have you.


Alice Wheeler
