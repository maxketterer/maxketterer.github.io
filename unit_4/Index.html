About


Willows & Wine is a pre-loved book store and wine bar serving hand-picked local and international wine, cocktails and spirits.

What started as an idea while travelling overseas became reality when my fiancé Ben and I, renovated the former Spanish Bodega in 2016 to create W&W.

Come by to read a book, play a board game or graze on artisan cheese platters and charcuterie boards with fresh with ingredients Queen Vic Market. We hope to see you soon. x


Alice Wheeler
